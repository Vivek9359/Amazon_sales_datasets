📊 Amazon Sales Datasets
📌 Overview
The Amazon Sales Dataset contains detailed records of product sales, customer transactions, and shipping information. It is designed for data analytics, visualization, and business intelligence projects, making it ideal for students, analysts, and professionals working with Excel and SQL.

📂 Dataset Information
File name: amazon_sales_dataset.csv

Format: CSV (Comma-Separated Values)

Content Includes:

Product details (ID, category, sub-category, description)
Customer information (region, demographics, purchase history)
Transaction data (order ID, date, quantity, price, discount, profit)
Shipping details (mode, delivery time, location)

🎯 Use Cases
Sales Analysis: Track revenue, profit, and discount impact.

Customer Segmentation: Group customers by purchase behavior.

Regional Insights: Compare sales across regions.

Forecasting: Predict future demand using historical data.

⚙️ Tools & Techniques
🟢 Excel
Pivot Tables for summarizing sales by product, category, or region.

Charts & Graphs (bar, line, pie) for visualizing trends.

Conditional Formatting to highlight top-performing products.

Data Cleaning using filters, text-to-columns, and formulas.
